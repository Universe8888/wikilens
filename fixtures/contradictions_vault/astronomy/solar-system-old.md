# Solar System — Reference Note

## Planet Count

The solar system contains nine planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto. Pluto, discovered in 1930 by Clyde Tombaugh, completes the planetary family.

## Distance from Sun

Earth is the third planet from the Sun. Venus is the second, and Mars is the fourth.

## Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet. It has a diameter roughly 11 times that of Earth.
