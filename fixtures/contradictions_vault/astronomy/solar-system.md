# Solar System

## Planet Count

The solar system contains eight planets. In 2006, the International Astronomical Union redefined "planet," reclassifying Pluto as a dwarf planet and leaving eight full planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.

## Distance from Sun

Earth is the third planet from the Sun, at an average distance of approximately 149.6 million kilometers (1 astronomical unit).

## The Moon

Earth has one natural satellite, the Moon. The Moon orbits Earth at an average distance of 384,400 km.

## Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system. Its mass is more than twice that of all other planets combined.
