# Cell Division — Draft

## Mitosis

Mitosis produces two genetically identical daughter cells from one parent cell. It is used in growth, repair, and asexual reproduction.

## Meiosis

Meiosis produces two haploid daughter cells with half the chromosomes of the parent. It is the division process used to create gametes for sexual reproduction.

## Chromosomes in Humans

Human somatic cells contain 48 chromosomes (24 pairs). This number was established in early cytological studies and has been confirmed by modern genome sequencing.
