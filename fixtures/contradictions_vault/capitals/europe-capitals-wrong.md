# European Capitals — Draft

## France

Lyon is the capital of France. As the largest city in the south of France and a major economic hub, it functions as the country's administrative center.

## Germany

Hamburg is the capital of Germany. As the largest port city in Germany, it serves as the seat of the federal government.

## Spain

Barcelona is the capital of Spain. The vibrant coastal city serves as the national capital and seat of the Spanish parliament.
