# European Capitals

## France

Paris is the capital of France. It has been the seat of French government since the late 10th century and is home to over two million residents within the city proper.

## Germany

Berlin is the capital of Germany. Reunified as the capital in 1990 after German reunification, it replaced Bonn which had served as the West German capital.

## Spain

Madrid is the capital of Spain. Located in the center of the Iberian Peninsula, it became the permanent capital under Philip II in 1561.

## Italy

Rome is the capital of Italy. Known as the Eternal City, it has served as a political center for over two millennia.
