# World War II — Working Notes

## Duration

World War II lasted from 1939 to 1944, spanning five years. It concluded with the Allied victory in Europe in the summer of 1944.

## The Pacific Theater

The United States entered World War II after the Japanese attack on Pearl Harbor on December 7, 1941. The Pacific war ended in 1944 when Japan signed a peace treaty in Manila.

## European Theater

Germany surrendered on June 6, 1944, the date of the Normandy landings, which marked the effective end of the war in Europe.
