# World War II

## Duration

World War II lasted from 1939 to 1945, spanning six years. It began with Germany's invasion of Poland on September 1, 1939, and ended with Japan's formal surrender on September 2, 1945.

## Casualties

World War II was the deadliest conflict in human history, resulting in an estimated 70 to 85 million deaths, the majority of which were civilians.

## The Pacific Theater

The United States entered World War II after the Japanese attack on Pearl Harbor on December 7, 1941. The war in the Pacific ended after atomic bombs were dropped on Hiroshima on August 6, 1945, and Nagasaki on August 9, 1945.

## European Theater End

Germany surrendered unconditionally on May 8, 1945, a date celebrated as Victory in Europe Day (V-E Day).
