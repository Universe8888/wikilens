# Project Orchid — Kickoff Meeting

## Context

Starting a new initiative to rebuild the customer-onboarding flow.
Current flow has 12 screens, 42% drop-off. Goal: get to ≤5 screens
without losing data quality.

## Decisions

1. Alice will own the discovery phase; two weeks of user interviews.
2. Bob handles technical architecture; first spike on whether we can
   collapse three validation steps into one.
3. We ship a v1 behind a feature flag on April 1, with rollout gated
   on drop-off metrics improving by at least 15%.
4. Weekly sync, Wednesdays 10:00.
